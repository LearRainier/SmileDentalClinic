About NewSmile

More than a clinic —
a community of smiles

We believe everyone deserves a healthy, confident smile. Since opening our doors, we've been committed to providing compassionate, high-quality dental care to the families of General Trias and beyond.

NewSmile Dental Clinic is a modern and friendly dental practice offering a wide range of dental services for patients of all ages. Whether you need a routine check-up, a filling, a root canal, or a cosmetic treatment, we have the expertise and equipment to provide you with the best possible care.

We believe that oral health is an important part of your overall well-being. That's why we use the latest technology and techniques to ensure your dental experience is comfortable and effective. We also educate our patients on how to maintain good oral hygiene and prevent dental problems in the future.

Our team of dentists, hygienists, and assistants are highly qualified and experienced. They are friendly and compassionate, making you feel at ease from the moment you walk in. We are committed to providing personalized, professional dental care that meets your needs and expectations.

We are conveniently located at Unit D Ground Floor, Hsinchu Bldg, Gov Ferrer Dr, General Trias, 4107 Cavite Philippines and open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
